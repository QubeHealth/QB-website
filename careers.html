HOW WE HIRE

We are very particular about the type of team (“crew”) we are trying to build here at Qube.

Not everyone gets to be a part of the team, and if they do, they have to prove to their team-mates that can make the whole team better, with the addition of skills and work ethic they can bring with them.

Our mission, is to change the way Indians pay for healthcare.

Paying for healthcare in India is a high-friction experience today. A small part of your payment is covered through your insurance - if you have it, and the balance is arranged through your own funds, or by borrowing from outside. We want to change this. We want to make it simple and easy. Giving Indians the power to choose the healthcare they need and deciding how to pay.

Our vision, is to be the only way to pay for healthcare in India.

If paying for healthcare is smooth through an App, giving you the power to choose if you want to pay from your bank account, or borrow quickly at no-cost, and earn discount cash-backs on every payment, why would you want to pay in any other way. Its the smart way to pay for healthcare.

Take control of the way you pay for healthcare. “Qube Karo!”
